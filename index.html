Who We Are

About Savannah Packs

Our Mission

Savannah Packs is a Nairobi-based eco-conscious brand specializing in handcrafted wooden lunch boxes. We focus on sustainability, durability, and timeless natural design that supports a greener future while offering premium everyday utility.

Our journey began with a simple question: why do we settle for plastic when nature offers something so much more beautiful? From that question, Savannah Packs was born — a brand that merges African craftsmanship with modern minimalist design.

Our Values

  • Sustainably sourced wood from responsible forests
  • Zero-waste production processes
  • Supporting local Nairobi artisans
  • Products designed to last decades
  • Packaging that's 100% recyclable
